About Me

She is nearly forty years old this year but still exudes youthful and radiant features
. We used to guess that when you were a teenager, you must have been very beautiful.
My first impression when meeting her is the grace and composure shown from actions
gestures, and voice. She has a slim, well-proportioned figure. She is not too tall,
when wearing shoes, her figure becomes harmonious and fitting. Her skin is pink
and white, full of life. When going to school, the outfit she wears the most is ao dai.
Those are the dresses she personally ordered, not too fussy but elegant, elegant,
exalting her lines and beauty. She walked very slowly, carefully, nothing could make
her hurry. Therefore, the image of her wearing a long dress with fluttering skirts and
walking on the school grounds is an unforgettable memory for each of her students.
Her face is oval in shape with full cheeks. Even though she had to wear glasses,
the glasses could not cover the warmth and kindness in her eyes.
